# Fees and Limits

Bridge usage may involve source-network gas fees and protocol-related processing behavior.

---

## What You Should Expect

When using the bridge, you may need to pay:

- Gas on the source network
- Token approval gas
- Transaction submission gas

---

## Important

Bridge conditions can vary depending on:

- Source network congestion
- Token approval requirements
- Confirmation timing
- Bridge design and relayer behavior

Always review transaction details in your wallet before confirming.

---

## Good Practice

Do not bridge your full balance on your first attempt.

Keep enough native gas on the source network to:

- Approve the token
- Submit the bridge transaction
- Retry if needed
