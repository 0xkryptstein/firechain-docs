# Report Issues

If you identify a possible issue, document it carefully before reporting it.

---

## Good Information to Collect

Useful details include:

- The exact page or service involved
- The wallet you used
- The network you selected
- The transaction hash if applicable
- The address involved
- The time the issue occurred
- Screenshots that do not expose secrets

---

## Never Share

Do not share:

- Seed phrases
- Private keys
- Full secret backups
- Sensitive wallet recovery material

---

## Best Practice

When reporting a contract or address issue, include the exact address and the official documentation page you were using.
