# Gas Fees

Gas fees are paid in FIRE and are required to process transactions on FireChain.

---

## When Gas Is Needed

You need FIRE for actions such as:

- Sending native tokens
- Swapping tokens
- Approving contracts
- Adding liquidity
- Deploying contracts
- Interacting with smart contracts

---

## Important

Even if you hold another token on FireChain, gas is still paid in FIRE.

Always keep a small FIRE balance in your wallet to avoid failed transactions caused by insufficient gas.

---

## Good Practice

When bridging or receiving assets, make sure you also retain enough FIRE for:

- A token approval
- A swap
- A transfer
- Contract interaction
