# FAQ

## What is FireChain?

FireChain is a blockchain ecosystem designed for scalable decentralized applications, digital assets, and cross-chain interoperability.

---

## What is Rinia?

Rinia is the current FireChain network environment documented in this portal.

---

## What token is used for gas?

FIRE is the native token used to pay gas fees on FireChain.

---

## Which wallets can I use?

You can use EVM-compatible wallets such as MetaMask, Rabby, and WalletConnect-supported wallets.

---

## Which networks are supported by the bridge?

The bridge currently supports:

- Ethereum Sepolia
- Base Sepolia
- Optimism Sepolia
- BNB Smart Chain Testnet
